About the Project

What gets left out of the historical record, and why?

That question is where this project started. Erased Histories is a student and faculty curated public history project documenting histories that have been removed, suppressed, or overlooked at any level and anywhere in the world. Students and faculty researched and wrote the entries you see here. The exhibit is free to explore and open to anyone. It grows as more contributors are added.

Some entries in this exhibit include descriptions and images of historical violence, racial injustice, and sexual violence. We have included this material because we believe these histories deserve to be seen and understood. Please engage at your own pace.

Hornet Histories

Your story belongs in the archive.

Hornet Histories is the participatory side of this project. Built around the idea that Sac State students carry stories worth preserving. Personal histories, family histories, community histories that have not been told yet.

You do not need to be a history major to contribute.
